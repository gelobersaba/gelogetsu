Special Features & Services

Table Service

Customers are seated at a table, and waitstaff takes their orders, serves the food, and attends to any requests throughout the meal.

Buffet Service

Customers serve themselves from a selection of dishes arranged on a buffet table.

Takeout/Delivery Service

Customers can order food to go and either pick it up themselves or have it delivered to their location.

Drive-Thru Service

Customers can place their orders without leaving their vehicles and receive their food through a designated drive-thru window.

Counter Service

Customers place their orders at a counter, pay for their food, and then either carry it to their table or take it to go.

Catering Service

Restaurants may provide catering services for special events, parties, and gatherings.
